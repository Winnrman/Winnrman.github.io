L E A F L E T

Leaflet is a blog / social media platform that I've been working on for a couple of years, designed around sharing what you want, and nothing you don't. Security is built in, and comes first. I designed it because I wanted a place to write about my life and keep a log of my actions, so I could look back on past events, kind of like a personal journal.

I've been inspired by other sites like Reddit and Tumblr, but I wanted to make a place for me to share my ideas where they would be safe and where it would be a positive experience to go onto the site. I've added countless features which are listed below, and I am constantly working on it to improve it and make it better for myself, with everyone else in mind.



Key Features

Security

Leaflet was designed with security in mind, so when you make a post private, it stays

private

and only you can see it.

Safety

Leaflet is designed to be safe, so you can't be a victim of a malicious post. If one is seen, it will be taken down and the user responsible will be banned.No exceptions.

Privacy

Leaflet is designed to be private, so you can't see what other people have posted if it's private. If you want to see what other people have posted, you have to be logged in and you'll only see posts they've made public.
